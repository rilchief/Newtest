The Challenge on the Ground

Smart agriculture offers a path to improve wheat productivity in the UK. However, farmers face a critical problem: optimizing nitrogen fertilizer use amidst unpredictable weather, without the real-time, localized data needed to make the right decisions.

This leads to environmental risks, economic waste, and reduced yields.
